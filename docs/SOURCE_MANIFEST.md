## Concrete usage notes

- The whitepaper and canonical PDF bundle were copied into `assets/seed_canon/` so `Seeded Eden` does not depend on the original desktop attachment locations.
- The attractor HTML/JSON examples directly informed the shipped basin export controls: play/pause, turn slider, attractor detail, and transition table.
- The Karkada et al. symmetry paper informed the geometry lab, ablation diagnostics, and the requirement to separate render layout from computed structure. It was not treated as proof of persona persistence.
- The GUI/TUI inspiration bundles informed the amber palette, framed boxes, structured logs, and the decision to keep the browser layer as export/inspection rather than the primary interaction loop.
- The Gephi GUI/workspace docs informed the browser refactor from a strip-dominant shell into a three-stage workbench with `Overview`, `Data Laboratory`, and `Preview`, plus docked panel roles for appearance, layout, statistics, filters, context, and inspection.
- The Gephi docs were used to bound the observatory's Gephi download menu to the import formats Gephi currently documents, and to keep topology-first versus richer-attribute export claims explicit.
- No `__MACOSX` or Finder metadata files were used.
